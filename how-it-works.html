How It Works

The "Find Your Micro:bit Friend" device uses a combination of micro:bit beacon and receiver programs to help users track proximity. The beacon micro:bit continuously emits a low-power radio signal, and the receiver micro:bit captures and measures this signal to indicate how close it is to the beacon.

The receiver's program maps the radio signal strength (ranging from -95 to -42) to a visual scale displayed on the micro:bitâ€™s LED matrix. The higher the signal strength, the more LEDs light up, showing proximity in real-time.

The device is especially useful for the hearing-impaired, offering a tactile and visual way to monitor the distance between individuals without relying on sound.
