Discover Your Micro Friend

Imagine trying to find your friends in a bustling crowd, surrounded with towering strangers. Its tough to connect! Our project, "Find your Micro Friend," leverages radio signals and the micro's LED display to empower hearing-impaired individuals with seemless proximity detection.



With visual cues guiding the way, you can effortlessly locate your friends, ensuring you never miss a moment together.



Feel the Connection and enjoy social interaction like never before!
