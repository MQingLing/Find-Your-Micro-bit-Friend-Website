FIND YOUR MICROBIT FRIEND

This project is designed to help the hearing-impaired stay connected through proximity detection using radio signals and visual cues on the micro:bit LED display.



Feel the Connection. Find Your Friend.

Benefits

"Find Your Micro:bit Friend" offers targeted navigation assistance to people with hearing impairments, providing a tactile approach for detecting the distance between familiar individuals. This can be particularly useful in social settings such as workplaces, or the common spaces where traditional visual or auditory cues may be inadequate. It makes Alerts and Social Interactions easier.



Stay connected, feel the distance, and never miss a moment!
