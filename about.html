About "Find Your Micro:bit Friend"

This project is designed to help the hearing-impaired stay connected through proximity detection using radio signals and visual cues on the micro:bit LED display.

Benefits

Find Your Micro:bit Friend provides tactile feedback and visual cues for individuals with hearing impairments, promoting better social interaction and safety awareness.
